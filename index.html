Summary: Latitude vs. X

For the data contained within this project, OpenWeather was queried to get weather data for over 500 cities around the world. Matplotlib was then used to draw various comparisons between various weather conditions and the latitude of each city. With that we hoped to find any correlations between latitude and inherent weather conditions.

The culmination of this project are the four visualisations found on this site. Each one compares latitude vs some weather aspect: temperature, cloudiness, humidity, and wind speed. You can either use the navigation bar at the top or the images at the right to navigate to the various parts of the site.
